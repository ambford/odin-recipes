Crepioca Recipe

Crepioca

You are going to learn how to prepare a delicious crepioca

Ingredients

  • 1 Eggs
  • Tapioca dough
  • Grated cheese
  • Filling

Steps

  1. Mix the dough and the eggs;
  2. After it is homogenous, add the chesse
  3. Pour the mixture into a warm pan so it spreads evenly;
  4. After 2 minutes, turn the dough araound in order to cook the other side;
  5. It's ready, now you can add the filling you want.
